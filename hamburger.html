A hamburger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked meat patty on a bun or roll. You can order a hamburger, fries, and a shake at most fast food restaurants. Hamburgers are traditionally made with ground beef and served with onions, tomatoes, lettuce, ketchup, and other garnishes.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheese Slice
  • Hamburger buns
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Prepare grill for medium-high heat
  2. Season ground beef and divide into balls
  3. Press each ball into patties
  4. Put the patties on the grill
  5. Add the cheese on top once browned
  6. Place the burgers on the bottom bun
  7. Add Lettuce and Onion on top
  8. Add the top bun
