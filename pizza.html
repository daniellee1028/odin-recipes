Pizza is a dish made typically of flattened bread dough spread with a savory mixture usually including tomatoes and cheese and often other toppings and baked

Ingredients

  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Bread Flour
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Kosher Salt
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Cornmeal

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Preheat the oven
  5. Divide dough into balls
  6. Prep the toppings
  7. Flatten the dough ball and stretch out into a round circle
  8. Brush dough with olive oil
  9. Sprinkle peel with cornmeal
  10. Spread tomato sauce and toppings
  11. Put in oven
